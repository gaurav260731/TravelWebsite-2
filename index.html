About Us

Ocean Maldives was founded with a vision to help tourists from all over the world discover the vibrant marine life, diverse islands, and rich culture of the Maldives. Our mission is to offer each traveler an authentic experience that goes beyond the typical tourist path.

  • We believe the Maldives is not just a honeymoon destination. It’s a land of adventure, culture, and hidden treasures waiting to be explored. Since our founding, Ocean Maldives has proudly served thousands of happy tourists from across the globe, delivering once-in-a-lifetime experiences.
  • Our Goal: To provide the best experiences while helping you uncover the hidden gems of the Maldives—all at affordable prices.

Our team is composed of passionate local hospitality professionals coming together to create unforgettable adventures tailored to your unique interests. Led by Ibbe (known as ibbe.donte on Instagram), who has over 10 years of experience in the industry, Ocean Maldives aims to showcase the true essence of Maldivian life—an opportunity many visitors miss out on.
